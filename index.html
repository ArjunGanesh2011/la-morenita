Our Story

A Latina Legacy
Rooted in Lincoln Heights

La Morenita has been a cornerstone of Lincoln Heights since the 1970s — a Latina-owned family business where every dish is made from scratch, every single day.

We believe in real food: no canned ingredients, no shortcuts. From our handmade tortillas pressed fresh each morning to our rich chiles rellenos simmered in house salsa — everything on your plate carries generations of love and tradition. Come as a stranger, leave as family.

Menu Highlights

Everything is made fresh every morning. No exceptions.

Chiles Rellenos

Poblano peppers stuffed with savory filling, battered in egg, and simmered in house-made salsa roja. A true signature dish.

Signature

Menudo

Slow-simmered tripe in a bold red chile broth with hominy — the Sunday morning tradition. Served with fresh handmade tortillas.

Weekend Tradition

Cecina Plate

Salt-cured beef, pan-seared to perfection. Served with fresh beans, rice, and warm handmade tortillas — generously portioned.

Our Story

A Latina Legacy
Rooted in Lincoln Heights

La Morenita has been a cornerstone of Lincoln Heights since the 1970s — a Latina-owned family business where every dish is made from scratch, every single day.

We believe in real food: no canned ingredients, no shortcuts. From our handmade tortillas pressed fresh each morning to our rich chiles rellenos — everything on your plate carries generations of love and tradition.
